Quick Setup

4 Simple Steps to Print

01

Pair Device

Go to Android system Bluetooth Settings, search for devices, and pair your thermal printer.

02

Scan & Link

Open the FFit BT app, tap on Scan, and connect to the paired printer to save the MAC address.

03

Enable Print Service

Go to Android Settings ➔ Print, find the FFit BT service, and toggle it to ON.

04

Select & Print

Inside any app (like Chrome), select Share ➔ Print, choose FFit BT, and tap print!

Features & Specs

Technical Specifications

Persistent Auto-Connect

Reads your printer's MAC address from local cache and automatically connects in the background whenever a print job starts. You don't need to open the app UI again.

Whitespace Margin Cropper

Intelligently scans document pages to crop off all empty spacing at the top and bottom. This prevents wasting valuable thermal paper on blank margins.

58mm & 80mm Support

Includes capabilities metadata for both 58mm (default, 384px resolution) and 80mm thermal sizes, guaranteeing that the system Print dialog never locks the Print button.

Standard ESC/POS Protocol

Translates Android print document bitmaps into high-performance raw raster commands. Appends a stylish brand footer: Powered by- FFIT.IO ♥
